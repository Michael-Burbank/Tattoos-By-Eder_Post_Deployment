About the Artist

My name is Eder Sanchez, a passionate and dedicated tattoo artist proudly serving clients at Firme Placas Tattoo Studio in Troy, Texas. With years of experience and a self-taught journey, I’ve honed my craft to specialize in Black and Grey Chicano Realism, a style that brings depth, emotion, and storytelling to life through ink.

My mission is to transform your vision into a masterpiece that not only complements your individuality but also stands the test of time. Whether it’s a bold statement piece or a finely detailed design, I’m here to collaborate with you and create art that resonates deeply. Let’s turn your skin into a canvas for unforgettable stories.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

HOW DO I BOOK AN APPOINTMENT?

To book an appointment, simply provide your name, tattoo details, reference images, and placement. A $100 non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment. The deposit will be applied toward the total cost of the tattoo.

NEEDING TO CANCEL / RESCHEDULE?

If you need to cancel or reschedule, a minimum of 48 hours in advance is needed to keep your deposit. If you cancel within 48 hours or no-show, the entire deposit is forfeited. You can reschedule up to 2 times with the deposit if notified more than 48 hours in advance.

HOW DO I MAKE MY DEPOSIT?

Deposits can be made with cash, CashApp, or Zelle. The remaining balance is due at the time of your appointment.

WHAT INFO IS REQUIRED FOR A QUOTE?

For quotes, please submit your full name, description of the tattoo, reference image / idea, approximate size (in inches), and photo of the requested area where you’d like the tattoo placed with a circle around it. This helps provide a clear understanding of your vision and quote.

CAN I BRING A GUEST?

You are welcome to bring up to one person to accompany you during your tattoo session. However, for your comfort, please keep the number of guests limited.

DO YOU OFFER COVER-UPS / FIX OLD PIECES?

I do not typically do cover-ups or fix other tattoos unless the design is small and very faded. If that’s the case, I will evaluate it and discuss possible solutions with you.
